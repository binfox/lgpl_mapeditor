Tips
  • Scrolling
    Press the mousewheel in the editor to move the complete map with your mouse or roll the map up/down with the mousewheel. Use the cursor keys to scroll very fast. On the right-bottom side of the screen, there is a blue button. Click and drag on this button, and you can move the complete map.

  • Tilepicker
    With the right mousebutton, you can pick a tile in an active layer (similar to Paintbrush). Now, you can draw this tile with the left mousebutton.

  • Save
    After you click the save-icon, a filebox will popup. If you hold the left-shift-key when clicking on the save-icon, you will bypass the filebox and immediately overwrite the opened file.
